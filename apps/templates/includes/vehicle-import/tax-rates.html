Important Notes
  • Vehicle age is calculated from manufacturing date to import date
  • VAT is calculated on the sum of CIF + Import Duty + Excise Tax
  • Environmental tax applies to all vehicles except motorcycles
  • Special rates may apply for commercial vehicles
  • Exchange rates are updated regularly
