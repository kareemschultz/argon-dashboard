This Calculator Considers:
  • Basic salary and various allowances
  • National Insurance Scheme (NIS) contribution
  • Medical and life insurance premiums
  • Personal allowances and deductions
  • Child deductions ($10,000 per child monthly)
  • GPSU membership fees
Key Tax Information:
  • Basic Tax Threshold: $100,000 monthly
  • Tax Rates:
    • 28% on first $2,400,000 of chargeable income
    • 40% on excess over $2,400,000
  • NIS Contribution: 5.6% of gross salary (max $15,680)
  • Insurance Cap: 10% of gross pay or $50,000 (whichever is less)

Note: This calculator provides an estimate based on the latest available information. Always consult with a tax professional or the Guyana Revenue Authority for the most up-to-date and personalized tax advice.
