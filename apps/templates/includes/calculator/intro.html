Income Tax Calculator (2024)

Calculate your income tax based on Guyana's current tax regulations.

This Calculator Considers:
  • Basic Salary Calculations
  • Taxable & Non-Taxable Allowances
  • National Insurance Scheme (NIS)
  • Personal Income Tax (PAYE)
  • Medical and Life Insurance
  • Child Tax Relief ($10,000 per child)
  • GPSU and Loan Deductions
  • Gratuity Calculations
Key Tax Information:
  • Personal Monthly Threshold:
    • Base: $100,000
    • Per Child: Additional $10,000
  • Tax Rates:
    • 28% on first $2,400,000 of chargeable income
    • 40% on excess over $2,400,000
  • NIS Contribution:
    • Rate: 5.6% of gross salary
    • Monthly Cap: $15,680
  • Insurance Deduction Cap:
    • 10% of gross salary, or
    • Maximum $50,000 monthly
Important: All calculations are based on monthly figures. Annual values are automatically computed where applicable.
