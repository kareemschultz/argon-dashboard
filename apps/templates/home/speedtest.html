What is Internet Speed Testing?

Internet speed tests measure the speed of your internet connection, providing insights into download and upload speeds as well as latency (ping). This information helps you understand the performance of your internet service.

Note: Results can vary based on various factors, including network congestion, time of day, and distance from the testing server.

Provided by OpenSpeedtest.com

Important Notes:
  • Please ensure you are not using other applications that may consume bandwidth during the test.
  • If the results seem inaccurate, try running the test at different times.
  • This speed test is meant for informational purposes only and should not be used as a definitive measure of your internet performance.
