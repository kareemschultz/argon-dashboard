What is Internet Speed Testing?

Internet speed tests measure your connection's performance across three key metrics:
  • Download Speed: How quickly you can receive data
  • Upload Speed: How quickly you can send data
  • Latency (Ping): Response time of your connection

Important Notes:

  • Close other bandwidth-consuming applications during the test
  • Results may vary based on network congestion and time of day
  • Test multiple times for more accurate results
  • This tool is for informational purposes only

Optimal Testing

For best results, use a wired connection or be close to your WiFi router. Test at different times to get a better picture of your connection's performance.

Understanding Results

Results can vary based on network congestion, server location, and your device's capabilities. Multiple tests provide more accurate averages.

Troubleshooting

If speeds are lower than expected, try restarting your router, checking for background downloads, or contacting your ISP for assistance.
